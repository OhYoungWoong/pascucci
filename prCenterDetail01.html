ACADEMY STORY

Happy Barista Academy with Pascucci (2nd Quarter)
Recently, there are many cafes where baristas with disabilities work.
As much as that, there are more and more talented baristas with disabilities!
That's why Pascucci's social contribution program for disabled baristas is the 'Happy Barista Academy'!
Time to give a solid education so that you can become a great barista even though you have a disability! Shall we meet you?

Aritcle Detail’s

  • # Passion that was as hot as the weather

    It is said that the Pascucci Training Center at SPC Future Creation Center was full of heatbecause there was coffee education for disabled barista trainees who love coffee.Shall we go to the hot scene?

  • # Pascucci's representative social contribution activity 'Happy Barista Academy'

    Pascucci's representative social contribution, 'Happy Barista Academy', is an activity that helps coffee education andpractice for barista trainees with disabilities who have a higher love for coffee than anyone else. Through the BaristaAcademy, it can be said that it is a social contribution to help trainees improve their coffee skills and help them findemployment.

  • # Espresso with sincerity

    After learning about coffee theory, it's time to practice. I tried making coffee using a 'moka pot' and a 'French press', but one of the trainees said, "I think coffee tastes better because I put so much effort into it." It even made me laugh when I said it ^^

  • # Did you make Pascucci sales drinks yourself ?!

    How to enjoy more delicious espresso made with sincerity?! It's Affogato! All you have to do is pour a little bit of espresso into Pascucci's latte gelato, and it is said to be the most delicious menu during this training.

  • # The level of difficulty is the highest, and the level of response is 1st! latte art

    Lastly, it is the 'Latte Art' time that trainees are waiting for the most. The great advantage of Pascucci'sHappy Barista Academy is that you receive one-on-one training from professional baristas, not just listening. The latte art practice mission this time was “let’s make a heart shape”. Even in the midst of repeated failures, little by little a heart shape begins to appear. Eventually, trainees became skilled enough to make a heart shape by themselves!

'Happy Barista Academy' with Pascucci 'Happy Barista Academy' with Pascucci has been held once a quarter since January 2014, Coffee education for disabled baristas at SPC Future Creation Center Pascucci Coffee Training Center. Pascucci's baristas participate as part of talent donation activities, espresso extraction, milk steaming, With various curriculasuch as aroma kit practice and latte art practice, it is loved by baristas with disabilities who participated in the training.
