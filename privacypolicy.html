Member IDs collected through cookies are used for the following purposes. (Cookies expire when you close your browser or log out.

However, if you refuse to install cookies, there may be restrictions on the provision of services.
  7. 5. Procedure and method of destruction of personal information
    • 1) Destruction procedure

    • 2) Destruction method
      -Personal information stored in electronic file format is deleted using a technical method that cannot reproduce the record.
      -Personal information printed on paper is shredded with a shredder or destroyed through incineration.
  8. 6. Provision of personal information to third parties

However, in the following cases, it is possible to provide personal information without the customer's consent in accordance with the relevant laws and regulations.

  10. 8. Protection of personal information for children under the age of 14

    The company checks the consent of the legal representative when collecting personal information of children under the age of 14, and proceeds with identity verification when exercising the right of legal representative. In this case, the information is used only for the consent of the information subject, and when requesting access to personal information and correction of errors, the necessary measures are taken without delay.

  11. 9. Rights of users and their legal representatives and how to exercise them
    • 1) Users may request to view and correct errors in their personal information at any time, and the company will take necessary measures without delay, and if there is a request for correction of errors, the relevant information will not be used until the correction is made.
    • 2) Also, you can contact the person in charge by e-mail or phone to confirm that you are a member and then apply for viewing/change/withdrawal of membership.
  12. 10. Technical/Administrative Protection Measures for Personal Information
    • The company ivs taking the following technical/administrative measures to ensure the stability of the collected personal information.
    • [Technical measures]
    • 1) Your personal information is protected by a password, and important data is protected by encrypting files and transmission data.
    • 2) In order to prevent leakage of customer's personal information due to hacking, etc., we use a separate security device (firewall, etc.) on the network and system side that blocks intrusion from outside to block and monitor intrusion.
    • 3) We are taking measures to prevent damage caused by computer viruses by using automatic vaccine and patch installation programs. The program is updated periodically, and in the event of a sudden virus outbreak, a patch is applied immediately to prevent invasion of personal information.
    • [Administrative measures]
    • 1) The company limits the right to access your personal information to a minimum number of people, and the corresponding persons are as follows.
      • -A person who performs marketing tasks directly with users
      • -Persons in charge of personal information management, such as the person in charge of personal information protection and the person in charge
      • -Others who are inevitably required to handle personal information for business
    • 2) To prevent personal information from being lost, stolen, leaked, etc. by the handler, awareness-raising and continuous education are provided.
    • 3) The handover of personal information-related handlers is thoroughly carried out in a state where security is maintained, and responsibility for personal information accidents after joining and leaving the company is clarified.
    • 4) Access is controlled by setting the computer room and data storage room as special protection areas.
    • 5) The company is not responsible for the user's personal mistake or for any incidents that occur due to the basic internet risk. Each member must properly manage his/her ID and password and take responsibility for it in order to protect his/her personal information.
    • 6) In the event that personal information is lost, leaked, altered, or damaged due to the mistake of an internal manager or an accident in technical management, we will notify the customer immediately and take appropriate measures and compensation.
