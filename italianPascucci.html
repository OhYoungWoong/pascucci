  • The history of Café Pascucci began in 1883 in Italy, the hometown of espresso,by the father and son of Pascucci, who are full of passion for coffee SINCE 1883 Antonio Pascucci's interest in green coffee At the end of the 19th century, Antonio Pascucci, who was trading between Italy and England, became very interested in green coffee among the items he traded. And we want to develop it. The history of Cafe Pascucci began with Antonio Pascucci's interest in green coffee beans.

  • In 1935, they started making their own coffee based in Monteccerignone. Antonio Pascucci's son, Mario Pascucci, along with his wife, Domenica, succeeded in experimenting with roasting beans in a tin pot to create new flavors and aromas in Montecerignone (a small town in the hills between Rimini and Pesaro). And the business of Cafe Pascucci will be developed in earnest.

  • In 1946, Pascucci introduced the technique of extracting espresso using equipment for the first time, introducing a new roasting machine and establishing a production line to create an environment capable of producing a large amount of high-quality coffee beans. 1946, Introduced the industry's first espresso extraction method using equipmentIn 1946, Mario Pascucci's younger brother, Dino Pascucci, introduced an espresso machine and introduced the industry's first espresso extraction method using equipment.

  • 1950's, Alberto Pascucci, the third generation of the Pascucci family, decided to industrializethe roasting process and installed the first production plant.

  • in 1963, By introducing roasting machines and establishing production lines for uniform roasting, we have created an environment where production of coffee beans can be expanded and high-quality coffee beans can be produced. So mass production became possible and we started delivering coffee beans using this truck.

  • Over the next 130 years, the efforts of the Pascucci family spread authentic Italian coffee and its culture to the world, and now it has grown into a global brand that has entered more than 100 cities in 20 countries, including Italy, the United Kingdom, and the United States. Pascucci is famous for being the first in Italy to blend a variety of coffee beans. Pascucci's beans go through a blending process with Pascucci's unique blending ratio with beans grown in each country's plantations with the best climatic conditions. With the goal of “Only Top-Quality Coffee,” we are producing good coffee beans by blending a total of 7 types of coffee beans.

  • Pascucci's efforts are to advance into more than 20 countries and 100 cities as an authentic espresso shop that is more faithful to the original image of Italy as a traditional Italian coffee brand.
