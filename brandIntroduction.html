  • In 1883, the story of Pascucci began. In Italy, the hometown of espresso and the birthplace of modern coffee, there lived Pascucci and his father, who were passionate about coffee. Through generations of coffee research, they were finally able to create Pascucci's unique coffee with a new taste and aroma. The Pascucci father and son started spreading their own coffee based in the nearby Monteccerignone region. In 1946, Pascucci introduced the technique of extracting espresso using equipment for the first time in the industry, and by introducing a new roasting machine and establishing a production line, it is also equipped with an environment capable of producing a large amount of high-quality coffee beans.

  • The efforts of the Pascucci family, which continued for 130years after that, grew into a global brand that spreadsauthentic Italian coffee and its culture to the world. Europe's coffee culture, which started in Italy, has spread to many countries around the world as well as the United States, becoming the beginning of the coffee shop culture we enjoy today. And, Pascucci, a traditional Italian coffee brand, is an authentic espresso shop that is more faithful to Italy's original appearance, and will advance into 40 cities in 20 countries. And in 2001, Pascucci met SPC in Korea.

  • Tradition, craftsmanship and know-how. It was a partner with so many things to do. SPC, which started as a small bakery in 1945, has been a traditional company that has led the Korean food industry and culture beyond confectionery and baking with the tenacity and persistence of its craftsmen. A competent Korean general food company that knows the Korean food industry and the shape of franchises, and has secured advanced dessert technology in France as a general food company, and an original coffee brand with authentic Italian espresso and coffee culture. So the meeting of these two may have been guaranteed the best partnership that creates good synergy from the start.

  • For a cup of traditional coffee, traditional warm dessert & food, more perfect service From the beginning, Korean Pascucci wanted to become a genuine brand that provides traditional products. Coffee is provided only from slow-roasted coffee beans that are meticulously managed, selected, and blended from the bean cultivation stage by the head office in Italy to preserve the original taste and aroma. With SPC's know-how, we have developed desserts & foods that are suitable for the domestic market and go well with authentic Italian espresso, and have created a menu that suits the tastes of Korean consumers. Considering the Korean cafe culture, which emphasizes gatherings and relaxation, the store is also delicately designed to feel lively with a spacious and pleasant space suitable for the Korean market.

  • 137-75, 18 years, successful! Now, Korean Pascucci has passed 18 years and is cruising within the Korean coffee market. More than 400 stores nationwide and steady sales growth prove that Pascucci is a true member of Korean coffee shop culture and a leading brand. Wouldn't our goal of providing new coffee, desserts, and food by combining the 137-year tradition of Italian coffee with the 75-year tradition of Korean food culture be successful?

  • For the next 19 years, 150 years...Korean Pascucci Thanks to the love of domestic customers, Pascucci has successfully settled in the Korean market. Now, beyond introducing authentic Italian espresso to customers who love Pascucci, we want to become a coffee shop with the originality of Korean Pascucci.So, we will become a brand loved for a long time in the Korean market.
