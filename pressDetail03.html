SPC Pascucci, 'Granita' color collaboration with Jevisco

  • Pascucci, operated by the SPC Group, announced on the 24th that it would carry out a 'Granita' color collaboration campaign with Gangnam Jevisco (000860), a domestic paint company.
  • This campaign is 'Color for me this summer! Under the theme of 'Find Your Summer Color' at Pascucci, trendy colors suitable for the summer season are applied to 'Granita', an Italian ice dessert, so that consumers can find their own color that suits their taste and personality. designed\ to find it.
  • 'Granita', which means 'to break ice', is a dessert originating from Sicily, Italy, and is a drink enjoyed by grinding fruit or coffee with ice. Pascucci's granita is characterized by adding a rich taste and coolness by adding gelato to a drink with crushed ice.
  • A total of 15 granitas with the colors proposed by Pascucci and Jevisco, which provides color solutions in various fields such as architecture and home appliances, introduce color stories and recommended color schemes for each drink. The representative product is △’Green Tea Granita (6,300 won)’ applied with ‘Granita Green’ color that provides stability and relaxation of the mind by containing the abundance of nature △It resembles a mango in the tropics and makes you feel sweet. 'Mango Yogurt Granita (KRW 6,500)' in 'Tropical Mango' color △ 'Plum Granita' (KRW 6,900), which creates a bright atmosphere with an attractive and lively 'Adorable Pink' point color.
  • The representative menu of the Espresso Bar Goyang Starfield Branch is the 'Espresso Set', which allows you to choose a cup of single espresso made with 'Golden Sack', a blend that symbolizes Pascucci, and one variety menu item.
  • Pascucci also carries out various promotions to commemorate the collaboration. From the 26th to June 8th, you can enjoy a 2,000 won discount when purchasing Granita through the Happy app. In addition, Granita Hour is operated from 3:00 pm to 4:00 pm on the third week of each month from June to August, and an additional glass of granita is given to all Happy Point customers who purchase granita during that time. proceed
  • An official from Pascucci said, “The color collaboration with Jevisco has resulted in a richer and more colorful granita.” We will continue various collaborations for new customer experiences.”
