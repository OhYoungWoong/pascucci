Pascucci, beach cake and two types of 'Haute Granita' released

  • (2022-07-27) Pascucci, an authentic Italian coffee shop run by SPC Group (Chairman Heo Young-in), announced on the 27th that it has released two new products limited to the summer season, a beach cake and 'Haute Granita'.
  • Under the theme of 'Find Your Summer (Find your summer at Pascucci!)', this new product is characterized by using various ingredients such as green apples and oat milk to enjoy cool and colorful flavors suitable for summer.
  • The main products are △Beach cake (KRW 32,000)’ with fresh green apple cream, fruit compote, and rich vanilla mascarpone cream between moist sheets △Vegetable oat milk ‘Oatly’ in harmony with savory black sesame and sea salt gelato 'Oat Black Sesame Granita (KRW 6,700)' where you can feel the sweetness and savory taste at the same time △ 'Oat Caramel Granita (KRW 6,700)', etc.
  • In particular, the 'Beach Cake' was inspired by Positano Beach, Italy's representative resort, and the seaside scenery was applied to the cake design to add a refreshing summer atmosphere. In addition, the two types of 'Oat Granita' are iced drinks made with oat milk and ice base, black sesame seeds and caramel served with gelato, providing a cool and sweet taste with a rich texture.
  • Meanwhile, Pascucci is holding various promotions for the month of August to celebrate the launch of new products. First of all, 1 cup of Americano (R) is provided for free when 5 stamps are accumulated in the Happy Point application (hereinafter referred to as Happy App) after purchasing manufactured beverages. In addition, if you make a pre-pickup reservation for whole cakes (reservation required 3 days before pick-up) through the Happy Order application (hereinafter referred to as Happy Order App), you can receive a coupon for 1 cup of Americano (R) and a 2,000 won benefit. More information on the event can be found on the Pascucci official website and the Happy app
  • An official from Pascucci said, “For consumers tired from the scorching heat, we have introduced differentiated seasonal products such as beach cakes that make you feel refreshed just by looking at them, and vegan granita made with oat milk.” Shiki will continue to release high -quality products and hold various events to enhance customer convenience and benefits.” (End)
