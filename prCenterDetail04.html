ACADEMY STORY

Happy Barista Academy with Pascucci
There are so many friends who have disabilities but are working hard to become great baristas.
That passion can be said to be superior to anyone else's! Pascucci Happy Barista Academy is
This is an activity to practice Pascucci's skills and coffee making practice so that you can become a better barista.
It is said that the competition for this academy was very high due to the high number of applications.
\ Shall we go to the happy barista academy that went through the fierce competition?

Aritcle Detail’s

  • # good luck!

    The academy started under the welcome of the instructor who always greeted trainees with loud applause and laughter. After the theoretical training, I received espresso extraction training using a moka pot. During manufacturing, you will be individually coached on the amount of coffee beans, water, and posture. It's the first time I've received training next to an instructor like this, and the instructor's words put a smile on his face when he said that he was very helpful..^^

  • # Mmm~, delicious!

    It's always delicious to eat carefully made by yourself~? After tasting the espresso extracted by grinding the coffee beans themselves, the first words of the trainees were “Mm~ delicious”. There is a trainee who says it is the most delicious espresso he has ever tasted. Really ^^? I was asked what kind of education I wanted to do the most. The trainees all say that they hope that the latte art time will come soon. Latte art time always seems to be popular.

  • # Waiting for latte art time

    As it was the most awaited time, the latte art training started, and the eyes of the trainees lit up again. A professional barista instructor trains each trainee one by one, right? Moreover, it is not an experiential type... I think the strength of 'Pascucci Barista Academy' is that you can treceive as much training as possible until you get the shape you want. Peak concentration to make a heart!! Repeated failures, but our trainees do difficult things. Cheers when you see the heart you made in turn! Today, some trainees challenged the advanced technique of rose shape.

  • This is how today's academy ended. There was a trainee friend who said he was worried about coming to the training because he was very shy. Pascucci will always support disabled barista friends who have a passion for coffee.

[Pascucci employees]
Kim Song-i, Jin Yeon-joo, Kim Jeong-woo, Lee Jin-young, Park Da-sol, Lee Yu-na, Kang Seo-won, Joo Yun-sun, Lee Eun-ho baristas

by. Pascucci

'Happy Barista Academy' with Pascucci 'Happy Barista Academy' with Pascucci has been held once a quarter since January 2014, Coffee education for disabled baristas at SPC Future Creation Center Pascucci Coffee Training Center. Pascucci's baristas participate as part of talent donation activities, espresso extraction, milk steaming, With various curriculasuch as aroma kit practice and latte art practice, it is loved by baristas with disabilities who participated in the training.
